About the Song

"Fix You" by Coldplay is a powerful anthem about resilience and finding hope in dark times. Released in 2005 as part of their album "X&Y", the song resonates with many listeners due to its emotive lyrics and soaring melody. Coldplay, a British rock band formed in 1996, is known for their heartfelt songwriting and dynamic performances.
